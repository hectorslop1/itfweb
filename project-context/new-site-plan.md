# New Site Plan: Ives Torres Foundation

## Project Goal

Build a new modern website for Ives Torres Foundation that is clean, professional, warm, bilingual, mobile-first, and easy to maintain.

The website should help visitors quickly understand:

1. Who the foundation is.
2. Who they help.
3. What programs and resources they offer.
4. How to attend events.
5. How to donate.
6. How to contact the foundation.

## Main Pages

### Home

Purpose: Create a strong first impression and guide users to the most important actions.

Sections:
- Hero with emotional message and primary CTAs
- What we do
- Featured programs
- Upcoming events
- Impact stats
- Community in action / Facebook highlights
- Donation CTA
- Resources preview
- Contact CTA

Primary CTAs:
- Need support
- View events
- Donate

### About

Purpose: Explain the foundation’s story, mission, vision, and values.

Sections:
- Story of the foundation
- Mission
- Vision
- Values
- Who we serve
- Team or leadership, if available

### Programs

Purpose: Present the main services/programs.

Programs may include:
- Padres con Poder
- Educational workshops
- Family support
- Self-advocacy
- Technology classes
- Inclusive community events
- Inland Empire
- Valle Central

### Events

Purpose: Show upcoming and past events in a clear way.

Features:
- Event cards
- Date, time, location
- Region
- Category
- Registration link
- Facebook link
- Past events section

### Resources

Purpose: Create a clear resource hub for families.

Categories:
- Special education / IEP
- Regional Centers
- Self-determination
- ABA
- Technology
- Videos
- Guides
- Reports
- External links

### Impact

Purpose: Build trust and show results.

Sections:
- Impact stats
- Testimonials
- Photos
- Partners
- Reports
- Stories of impact

### News

Purpose: Share updates, stories, community events, and announcements.

Features:
- News cards
- Categories
- Featured posts
- Individual article pages

### Donate

Purpose: Make donating clear, easy, and trustworthy.

Sections:
- Donation message
- PayPal donation button/link
- Zelle instructions
- Suggested donation amounts
- Donation impact examples
- Nonprofit/tax info
- FAQ

### Contact

Purpose: Let people contact the foundation easily.

Sections:
- Contact information
- Contact form
- Appointment request option
- Reason selector
- Social links

### Language

The site should support Spanish and English.

Preferred structure:
- Spanish as default
- English available through a clear ES / EN switch

## Version 1 Scope

Included:
- Modern responsive website
- Main pages
- CMS for events, news, resources, Facebook highlights, and site settings
- PayPal/Zelle donation section
- Contact form
- Facebook highlights section
- Basic SEO
- Cloudflare Pages deployment

Not included in v1:
- User login
- Family portal
- Custom event registration system
- Custom payment processing
- CRM
- Advanced automation
